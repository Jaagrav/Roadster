Controls

  • Use W,A,S,D or Arrow Keys or the Joystick in the right bottom corner to control car.
  • Press R in case the car flips over or is stuck. In some cases you might need to refresh the page.
  • Press Space Bar to brake.
  • Change colors by clicking on them on the color palette.
  • Drag to orbit around the car and scroll to zoom.
